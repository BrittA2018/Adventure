Hello and welcome to my page! My name is Brittany Arambulo and this is the site that i created with inspiration from all my adventures! I've created a bucket list of all the pulsifying, adrenaline rush adventures i care to seek. Just last year in 2017 i decided my new year resolution would be to create a list no matter how long or short, of all the things i wanted to do out of my comfort zone and to enjoy what life truly brings to us that most people aren't able to take the time to enjoy it to the fullest. I have many adventures left to explore and many goals to fulfill as of yet but as i check off each adventure off my list, i grow more connected to my inner daredevil self and let me tell you, it is most gratifying. I encourage everyone to make a list, search for your happiness and enjoy what good life itself can bring to the soul. So please feel free to check out my site and maybe you will find some common interests that maybe you would dare to try! Thanks for visiting my page and good luck on your uplifting journey yet ahead!
